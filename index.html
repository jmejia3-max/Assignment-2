Introduction

The first Air Jordan 1 was released in 1985 by Nike. The shoes were designed for basketball star Michael Jordan when he started playing in the National Basketball Association.

At the time, the NBA had rules about shoe colors. The black and red Air Jordans did not follow those rules, so Michael Jordan was fined when he wore them during games. Nike paid the fines and used the controversy to promote the sneakers.
