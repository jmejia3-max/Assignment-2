Introduction

The first Air Jordan 1 was released in 1985 by Nike. The shoes were designed for basketball star Michael Jordan when he started playing in the NBA.

The NBA originally banned the black and red colorway, but Nike paid the fines and used the controversy to promote the sneakers.
