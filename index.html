The History of Air Jordan Sneakers

The first Air Jordan 1 was released in 1985 by Nike. The shoes were designed for basketball star Michael Jordan when he started playing in the National Basketball Association. At the time, the NBA had rules about shoe colors. The black and red Air Jordans did not follow those rules, so Jordan was fined when he wore them in games. Instead of stopping him, Nike paid the fines and used the controversy to promote the shoes.

The Air Jordan 1 was the first model in the series and was designed specifically for Michael Jordan. It was released in 1985 and quickly became a cultural icon.
