Popularity in Culture

Air Jordans became popular not only in basketball but also in streetwear and hip-hop culture. Limited releases make them rare and valuable.

Today many collectors treat Air Jordans as fashion items and investments.

Air Jordan Timeline

  1. 1985 – Air Jordan 1 released
  2. 1988 – Air Jordan 3 introduces the Jumpman logo
  3. 1997 – Jordan Brand becomes its own division
  4. Today – Air Jordans are popular worldwide
