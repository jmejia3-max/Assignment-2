Popularity in Culture

Air Jordans became popular not only in basketball but also in streetwear and hip-hop culture. Many fans collect different models because limited releases make them rare and valuable.

Today, Air Jordans are considered both fashion items and collectibles. Collaborations with designers and brands increase their value and popularity.

Air Jordan Timeline

  1. 1985 – Air Jordan 1 released
  2. 1988 – Air Jordan 3 introduces the Jumpman logo
  3. 1997 – Jordan Brand becomes its own division
  4. Today – Air Jordans are popular worldwide
