Why Air Jordans Are So Popular

Air Jordans became popular not just in basketball but also in streetwear and hip-hop culture. Many fans collect different Air Jordan models because new designs and limited releases make them valuable. The brand later became Jordan Brand, which continues releasing new sneakers every year. Today, Air Jordans are some of the most famous sneakers in the world.

People collect Air Jordans today because they are considered status symbols and investment pieces. The limited releases and collaborations with designers and brands increase their desirability and value over time.

Air Jordan Timeline

  1. 1985 – Air Jordan 1 released
  2. 1988 – Air Jordan 3 introduces the Jumpman logo
  3. 1997 – Jordan Brand becomes its own division
  4. Today – Air Jordans are popular worldwide
